Common Questions

Answers before the conversation.

Do you work with clients outside India?
Yes. While we're based in Nashik, we work with clients worldwide. Most project work is conducted remotely with structured milestone check-ins, and we travel for on-site discovery when needed.
How do you handle post-delivery support?
We don't hand over and disappear. Every engagement includes a stabilisation period post-launch, and we offer ongoing retainer-based support for maintenance, iteration, and feature additions. We stay until your team fully owns the system.
Can you work within our existing systems (ERP, EMR, tools)?
Absolutely. We frequently integrate with existing infrastructure — legacy ERPs, hospital information systems, third-party APIs, and IoT hardware. Our process begins with mapping what you already have before proposing anything new.
What's the typical engagement size or duration?
Projects range from 4-week automation builds to 6-month full-system implementations. We work with SMEs, mid-size enterprises, and institutional clients. We're GST-registered, invoice formally, and can support vendor registration requirements for hospital and government procurement processes.
