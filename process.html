How We Work

A method built on
listening before building.

We don't start with a technology stack. We start with the person at the end of the process — and work backwards to the right solution.

The Engagement Model

Five stages. No surprises.

01
Discover
We immerse in your domain — workflows, users, regulations, and pain points before proposing a single feature.
In healthcare, this means understanding clinical workflows before a single screen is drawn.
02
Map
Real-world processes translated into precise system blueprints — user journeys, data flows, integration points, edge cases.
For manufacturers, shop-floor constraints meet software architecture here.
03
Design
Interfaces designed for the actual user — not a generic persona. Every screen reviewed against real workflows.
04
Build
Clean, scalable, documented code. Iterative delivery with client checkpoints — no big-bang surprises at handoff.
05
Support
Post-launch partnership — training, iteration, and ongoing support. We stay until your team fully owns the system.
Common Questions

Answers before the conversation.

Do you work with clients outside India?
Yes. While we're based in Nashik, we work with clients worldwide. Most project work is conducted remotely with structured milestone check-ins, and we travel for on-site discovery when needed.
How do you handle post-delivery support?
We don't hand over and disappear. Every engagement includes a stabilisation period post-launch, and we offer ongoing retainer-based support for maintenance, iteration, and feature additions.
Can you work within our existing systems?
Absolutely. We frequently integrate with existing infrastructure — legacy ERPs, hospital information systems, third-party APIs, and IoT hardware. Our process begins with mapping what you already have.
What's the typical engagement size or duration?
Projects range from 4-week automation builds to 6-month full-system implementations. We work with SMEs, mid-size enterprises, and institutional clients. We're GST-registered and invoice formally.
How do you price your engagements?
Engagements are priced based on scope, complexity, and duration — not per-hour rates. We provide a clear breakdown after the discovery call so you know exactly what you're paying for before we start.
Do you sign NDAs and formal contracts?
Yes. We're a GST-registered firm operating professionally. NDAs, SoWs, and formal agreements are standard. We invoice formally with HSN codes and full compliance documentation.
